## mimic_safemimic

goal: try to duplicate the video parsing pipeline of the paper SafeMimic by Bahety et. al.

Challenge: The code is not live yet. I have to reverse engineer it.

from the paper they used:
 - Body Tracker: Vickie Ye, Georgios Pavlakos, Jitendra Malik, and Angjoo Kanazawa. Decoupling human and camera motion from videos in the wild.
 - Hand Tracker: Georgios Pavlakos, Dandan Shan, Ilija Radosavovic, Angjoo Kanazawa, David Fouhey, and Jitendra Malik. Reconstructing hands in 3D with transformers.
 - Contact Detector: Dandan Shan, Jiaqi Geng, Michelle Shu, and David Fouhey. Understanding human hands in contact at the internet scale.
 - VLM for segmentation: ChatGPT o4
